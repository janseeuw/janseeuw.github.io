Prevent long response time with free tier Heroku apps

16 December 2014

As you may have noticed, Heroku apps with a free tier plan suspend after one hour of inactivity. Visitors will experience a long response time when they visit your web application.

Though "pinging" web monitoring services are supposed to be a workaround for making sure that your app never idles. HTTP caching can be a more appropriate solution to get faster response times.

Heroku doesn't provide HTTP caching by default, but we can instead use a CDN like Cloudflare or Amazon CloudFront. Cloudflare is free and easy to setup in a couple of minutes. Oh, and you also get free SSL.
