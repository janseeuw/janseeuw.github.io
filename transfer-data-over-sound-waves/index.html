Transfer data over sound waves

09 August 2014 on Android, Mobile Development

I was curious if it was possible to transfer data between mobile devices over sound. I took the lead in a group project to build an Android app that could transfer short messages between devices over sound waves. I was responsible for the modulation and demodulation techniques. We successfully transferred messages between devices, yet speed was a big bottleneck.

Jonas Anseeuw

PhD Student at Ghent University
